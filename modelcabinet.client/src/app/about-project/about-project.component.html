About ModelCabinet

Learn more about the purpose of ModelCabinet, the contributors, and its features.

What Problem Does ModelCabinet Solve?

ModelCabinet is designed to simplify 3D model management by providing tools for file organization, sharing, and collaboration. With its user-friendly interface, ModelCabinet allows users to upload, view, and download STL files effortlessly.

Key Features

  • Cross-Platform Compatibility: Access ModelCabinet on mobile, desktop, and web.
  • 3D Collaboration: Share designs effortlessly with others.
  • Secure Storage: Your 3D models are safely stored and easily accessible.
  • Intuitive UI: Enjoy a smooth user experience with an easy-to-use interface.
