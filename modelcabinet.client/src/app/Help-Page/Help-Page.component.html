Help Page for ModelCabinet

Find guidance on using ModelCabinet, troubleshooting common issues, and accessing support resources.

What is ModelCabinet?

ModelCabinet is a tool designed to manage 3D models efficiently. It offers features for organizing files, sharing with collaborators, and facilitating teamwork in 3D design projects.

Getting Started

To begin using ModelCabinet, create an account and explore the user-friendly interface. Upload your STL files and start managing your 3D models today!

Common Issues

  • File Upload Errors: Ensure your file is in the correct format (STL) and does not exceed the size limit.
  • Access Issues: Check your internet connection and ensure you are logged in to your account.
  • Collaboration Problems: Make sure that the users you are trying to share with have valid accounts and permissions.

Need Help?

If you require further assistance, please reach out to our support team.

Contact Support
